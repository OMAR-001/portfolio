Who am I ?

Hello! I'm Omar el khanati, a passionate and creative designer born on December 12, 2001. My expertise lies in fantasy photo manipulation and graphic design, specializing in logos and captivating social media ads using Photoshop and Illustrator.


I embarked on my design journey by transforming ordinary images into extraordinary art. I'm dedicated to pushing the boundaries of imagination, crafting visually stunning compositions that range from mythical scenes to modern logos.


In 2019, I completed my baccalaureate, followed by a diploma in specialized multimedia development in 2022. My quest for knowledge led me to explore various online courses, including logo design, soft skills, and even Python programming, enhancing my creative toolkit.


Beyond design, I find joy in composing music, immersing myself in captivating books, and seeking inspiration in every corner of life. With each project, I strive to elevate my craft and embrace the limitless possibilities within the realm of design.
